ABOUT THE LABEL

The Imaginary Records is an independent record label born from the mountains of Kashmir — built by artists, for artists.

We sign, develop, and distribute artists globally across hip hop, dark trap, lo-fi, UK drill, and beyond. Our model puts creative control in the hands of the artist, not the label.

Whether you are a solo producer dropping instrumentals or a vocalist building your catalog, TIR provides the infrastructure, distribution, and industry network to take your music worldwide.
