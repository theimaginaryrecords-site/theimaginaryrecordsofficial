1. Introduction

The Imaginary Records ("TIR", "we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website at theimaginaryrecords.in and use our services.

Please read this policy carefully. If you disagree with its terms, please discontinue use of our site.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you:

  • Submit a demo or contact form
  • Register as an artist on our platform
  • Submit music releases via our artist portal
  • Contact us via email or social media

This may include your name, email address, phone number, music files, artist information, payment details, and any other information you choose to provide.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process and distribute your music to streaming platforms
  • Communicate with you about your releases, royalties, and label activities
  • Process royalty payments to you
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our agreements
  • Improve our services and website
  • Send you updates and marketing communications (with your consent)

4. Sharing of Information

We may share your information with third parties only in the following situations:

  • Distribution Partners: PDL India (Phonographic Digital Limited) and Conyfer, our distribution partners, receive necessary metadata and files to distribute your music.
  • Streaming Platforms: Spotify, Apple Music, Amazon Music, and other DSPs receive artist and release metadata as part of distribution.
  • Payment Processors: PayPal and other payment processors for royalty disbursements.
  • Legal Compliance: When required by law, court order, or governmental authority.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Our platform uses Firebase/Google Cloud infrastructure with industry-standard security.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your artist account remains active or as needed to provide services. Upon account termination, we retain data as required by law or for legitimate business purposes for up to 7 years.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to: access your personal data; correct inaccurate data; request deletion of your data (subject to legal obligations); object to processing; and withdraw consent. To exercise these rights, contact us at support@theimaginaryrecords.in.

8. Cookies

Our website may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience. You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn we have collected such information, we will delete it promptly.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

The Artist retains 100% ownership of their master recordings and publishing rights at all times. TIR does not claim ownership of any content.

Section 3 — Royalty Split

Net royalties received by TIR from distribution partners shall be split as follows:

  • Artist receives: 95% of net royalties
  • Label retains: 5% as a distribution and services fee

"Net royalties" means gross income received by TIR from distribution partners, minus any platform fees, currency conversion charges, or withholding taxes imposed by third parties beyond TIR's control.

Section 4 — Distribution Timeline

TIR commits to submitting approved releases to distribution platforms within 24 to 48 hours of receiving complete, compliant release materials. Platform processing times may vary; typical live date is 1–5 business days from submission. TIR is not liable for delays caused by platform processing queues.

Section 5 — Lock-In Period

Artists agree to a 6-month minimum commitment from the date of first release under TIR. During this period, the Artist may not distribute the same content through competing services without prior written consent from TIR. After 6 months, either party may terminate with 30 days' written notice.

Section 6 — Royalty Payments

Royalties are processed and paid monthly, typically within 45–60 days of the close of each reporting period (as determined by our distribution partners). Payments are made via PayPal or other agreed methods. Artists must maintain valid payment information. Unclaimed royalties will be held for up to 12 months before being forfeited.

Section 7 — Royalty Disputes

The Artist has a 30-day window from the date of each royalty statement to raise disputes. Disputes must be submitted in writing to support@theimaginaryrecords.in with specific details. TIR will investigate and respond within 15 business days. After the 30-day window, statements are deemed accepted.

Section 8 — Copyright Claims

If a copyright claim, Content ID dispute, or takedown notice is received by TIR related to the Artist's content, TIR will notify the Artist via email within 48 hours of receiving the notice. The Artist must respond with counter-claim documentation within 7 business days. Failure to respond may result in acceptance of the claim and removal of the affected content.

Section 9 — Artist Warranties

The Artist warrants and represents that:

  • They own or control all rights to the submitted music, including master recording rights, composition rights, and sample clearances
  • The music does not infringe on any third-party intellectual property rights
  • All featured artists, producers, and contributors have provided written consent for the release
  • The music does not violate any laws or regulations
  • All metadata provided (artist name, album name, ISRC, etc.) is accurate and truthful

Section 10 — Prohibited Activities & Fraudulent Conduct

The following activities are strictly prohibited and constitute material breach of this Agreement, subject to immediate penalties:

  • Stream Fraud: Using bots, clickfarms, automated tools, or any artificial means to inflate streaming counts, listener numbers, or playlist placements

  • Identity Fraud: Submitting music under a false artist name or impersonating another artist
  • AI-Generated Content Fraud: Submitting AI-generated music while falsely representing it as human-created where platforms prohibit AI content, or without required AI disclosure
  • Metadata Fraud: Providing false ISRC codes, UPC codes, or metadata to manipulate chart positions or royalty calculations
  • Platform Manipulation: Any activity designed to manipulate DSP algorithms, editorial playlists, or recommendation systems through fraudulent means
  • Label Impersonation: Using TIR's name, logo, or brand without authorization
  • Duplicate Releases: Submitting the same content through multiple distributors simultaneously without TIR's written consent

  • Full indemnification: The Artist shall be liable for 100% of all costs, legal fees, fines, damages, and losses incurred by TIR as a direct result of the Artist's violation, including but not limited to: distribution partner penalties, DSP bans, legal representation fees, court costs, and reputational damages
  • Reporting to authorities: TIR reserves the right to report fraudulent activity to Indian law enforcement, distribution partners (PDL India, Conyfer), and relevant DSPs
  • Blacklisting: The Artist may be permanently blacklisted from all TIR services and from distribution through TIR's partner networks
